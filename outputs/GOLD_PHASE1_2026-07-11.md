## Bug 4 (VERIFY) — CalendarTom P&L units  [CLAIM CONFIRMED, FIXED]

Traced end-to-end: `CalendarTomEngine.hpp:224` emitted
`pnl = price_bp/1e4 * (lot*usd_per_pt)` = (exit-entry)/entry × lot × usd_per_pt.
The ledger contract (trade_lifecycle.hpp Step 1) multiplies tr.pnl by
tick_value_multiplier(symbol) — engines must emit RAW pts×lot. Net booked USD was
therefore `correct × usd_per_pt/entry`:
- XAU example: entry 4000 → exit 4040, lot 0.05. Correct: 40×0.05×100 = **$200.00**.
  Old emission: (40/4000)×0.05×100 = 0.05 → ×100 = **$5.00** (40x understated).
- US500: understated ~120x (entry/50). All 6 TOM instances affected. The
  double-mult backstop never caught it (ratio 0.025 « 0.7×mult).
Review's "understates ~by the gold price" — confirmed (exactly entry/usd_per_pt).

Fix: emit RAW `(exit-entry)*lot`; ledger applies mult + recomputes net from
spreadAtEntry (already populated). The wrong engine-side cost/net_pnl (also /entry
mis-scaled) removed — apply_realistic_costs overwrites net_pnl anyway.
